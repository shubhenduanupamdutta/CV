I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in Interdisciplinary Centre for Energy Research in Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. I am currently focusing on dwell fatigue behaviour of alpha titanium alloys, specifically Ti-5Al-2.5Sn ELI grade material. I am also concurrently working on fatigue behaviour of shockwave deformed material and failure of additive manufactured materials.

My Skills.

Material Characterization

I am proficient in using different methods of material characterizations using electron microscopy such as SE, BSE imaging, EBSD, X-Ray Diffraction and Bulk Texture. I am well versed in various other modes of characterization such as hardness measurements, DSC etc. I have also been doing multiple types of fracture surface analysis. I am also familiar with multiple other ways of characterization such as TEM, auger electron microscopy, AFM and nano-indentation.

Programming in Python

I am quite used to doing programming in python. I needed to modify or create programs to parse large amount of data and model the material deformation mechanisms. I have been mainly focuses on pandas and matplotlib libraries. However, I also have working knowledge of machine learning and web development and API libraries of python.
